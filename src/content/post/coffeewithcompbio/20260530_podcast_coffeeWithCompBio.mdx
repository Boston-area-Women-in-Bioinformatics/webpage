May is mental health awareness month and we're highlighting neurodiversity with an open and insightful conversation centered on lived experience and understanding. In this episode, our co-host Dina Issakova sits down with two guests [Isabella Chawrun](https://www.felskylab.com/) and [Lorena Pantano](https://www.linkedin.com/in/lpantano/) who share their experiences with ADHD and autism. Together, they discuss challenges, strengths, misconceptions, diagnosis journeys, and what neurodiversity looks like in everyday life and Bioinformatics.  
Join us for an honest conversation about mental health, self-advocacy, and creating a more inclusive world all with a cup of coffee.
