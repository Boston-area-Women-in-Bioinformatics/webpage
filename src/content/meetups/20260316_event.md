## Details:

​Celebrate the arrival of spring with your fellow bioinformatics besties! It’s been six weeks since Groundhog Day, which means winter is finally behind us. What better excuse to grab lunch together?!

​Whether you're already part of our community or hoping to make new connections, join us for a relaxed lunch where women and all gender minorities can meet, mingle, and share ideas over good food and great conversation. Let’s shake off the winter chill and step into the new season with inspiring discussions, meaningful networking, and a little springtime spirit.

### What to Know Before You Register

- This gathering is intended for individuals who identify as <span class="underline decoration-double">[women](https://www.thetrevorproject.org/resources/category/gender-identity/)</span>. 👩‍🔬
- Each guest will be responsible for purchasing their own meal and/or beverages. 🍽️

### How to find us

For those driving, parking is available at the 350 Kendall Street Garage (Pilgrim Parking). If you’re taking the T, Shy Bird is just a short walk from the Kendall/MIT station.
