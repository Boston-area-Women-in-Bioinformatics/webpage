Join the Pharmaceutical & Bioscience Society (PBSS) and Women in Bio for a free full-day webcast on the latest advances in therapeutics and diagnostics for women's health. Nine speakers from across academia, biotech, and industry will share their work spanning oncology, autoimmune disease, neurology, reproductive health, and more.

## Speakers

- **Sara Kenkare-Mitra, PhD** — ex-SVP Genentech, ex-President & Head of R&D, Alector
- **Kate J. Stephenson, PhD** — Cern Device
- **Laura Esserman, MD, MBA** — UCSF Breast Care Center
- **Meredith Hartley, PhD** — University of Kansas
- **Caroline Jefferies, PhD** — Cedars-Sinai Medical Center
- **Julie Andersen, PhD** — Buck Institute
- **Ridhi Tariyal, MBA, SM** — NextGen Jane
- **Hong Liu, PhD** — Bill & Melinda Gates Medical Research Institute
- **Diana Dou, PhD** — Duke University School of Medicine

Registration is free. The event runs 8:30 AM–2:30 PM Pacific (11:30 AM–5:30 PM Eastern).
