Are you looking to connect with others in your field in a fresh, dynamic, and innovative environment? Join us for CIC's Open Work & Collaborative Days, your opportunity to explore our office and event spaces, experience our vibrant energy and ecosystem, meet members of our incredible team, and access exclusive programming and meaningful connections.

This particular session is designed specifically for women in technology, creating space for connection, collaboration, and community among women across the tech ecosystem. We're hoping to bring together founders, engineers, product leaders, investors, creatives, and technologists for a day of coworking, organic networking, and intentional programming.

**Hours:** 8AM–4PM, followed by [Venture Café Thursday Gathering](https://venturecafecambridge.org/) from 4:30–8PM.
