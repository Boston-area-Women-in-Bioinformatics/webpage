Pack your sunscreen because Boston Women in Bioinformatics is hosting an outdoor hangout at Danehy Park in Cambridge this summer! Whether you're flying solo or bringing the whole crew, soak in the fresh air and catch up with your fellow bioinformatics peers.

## Getting There

Free parking is available right at the park. If you're taking the T, the Alewife Red Line stop is a short walk away. Look for us near the Sherman Playground and the Splash Pad!
