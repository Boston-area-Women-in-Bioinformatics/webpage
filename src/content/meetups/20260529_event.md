## Details

Tired of eating lunch alone at your desk? Come share a meal with fellow bioinformatics professionals where great conversations, shared ideas, and new connections are on the menu!

Whether you're navigating a big pharma pipeline, building out a small biotech's entire bioinformatics function, or doing it all yourself as a contractor, this is your chance to swap ideas, swap contact info, and maybe finally meet the person behind that blog you bookmarked six months ago.

Bring your takes on the latest in single-cell workflows, LLM-powered analysis, multi-omics integration, or whatever rabbit hole you've fallen into lately. No tokens necessary.
