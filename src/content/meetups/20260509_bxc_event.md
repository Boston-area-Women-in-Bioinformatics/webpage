[Sarosh Effendi](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sarosh-effendi/) has spent over 20 years working across some of the most complex environments in biopharma. He started in regulatory and drug safety, pivoted into program leadership, and has since operated across portfolio strategy, academic alliances, and cross-functional execution at an enterprise scale at Celgene, BMS, Rocket Pharmaceuticals, and AstraZeneca.

Join Bioinformatics XChange for a talk by Sarosh on navigating career transitions in biopharma.

He'll share a candid perspective on what drove his pivots, how he built credibility across functions, and what it takes to grow a career that spans science, strategy, and people leadership.
