**[Tommy Tang](https://www.linkedin.com/in/%F0%9F%8E%AF-ming-tommy-tang-40650014/), Director of Bioinformatics at AstraZeneca**, has spent 800+ days posting daily on LinkedIn. The results: inbound interest from VCs, founders, and advisory board invitations. In this talk, he breaks down the three habits behind it: consistent social media presence, blogging, and building the right professional connections.

Expect practical tactics most scientists don't hear about: what to post, how to tell your story, and why that blog from 10 years ago still matters.
