Are you a bioinformatician in the Boston or Cambridge area? Come join us for a casual evening of networking and conversation.

Whether you're running variant calling pipelines, doing RNA-seq analysis, working in structural biology, or just trying to figure out why your AWS bill is so high — this event is for you.

## What you get

- First drink on us
- $250 in Carolina Cloud compute credits
- A chance to connect with other bioinformaticians and computational biologists in the area

## Who should come

- Bioinformaticians and computational biologists
- Genomics researchers and data scientists
- Lab directors and PIs working with sequencing data
- Anyone running bioinformatics pipelines in the cloud or thinking about it

## What is Carolina Cloud?

Carolina Cloud is a cloud compute provider built specifically for bioinformatics and life sciences. We offer on-demand dedicated instances with no egress fees, and one-click genomics environments at a fraction of the cost of AWS. Our team has deep experience in genomics and computational biology, and we provide direct technical support from the founders.
