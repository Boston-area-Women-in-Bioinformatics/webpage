Swap your terminal for a barstool and join us for a casual happy hour where bioinformaticians (and bioinformatics-curious folks) come together to laugh, swap stories, and make the kinds of connections that don't require a p-value.
Come as you are. Leave with new friends, new ideas, and maybe a few good recommendations for your next pipeline.

### What to Know Before You Register

- Each guest will be responsible for purchasing their own meal and/or beverages. 🍽️

### How to find us

For those driving, parking is available at the 222 Jacobs Street Garage, or at the Cambridgeside Mall. If you’re taking the T, Tap 151 is just a short walk from the Lechmere green-line station.
