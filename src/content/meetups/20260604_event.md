Join us for an energizing discussion and networking session designed to foster meaningful connections among women working in bioinformatics and related fields. Whether you're a student, researcher, data scientist, or industry professional, this is a unique opportunity to expand your network, share experiences, and spark collaborations in a supportive, women-led environment.

You'll hear from the Boston Women in Bioinformatics group and meet peers and mentors from diverse backgrounds, each bringing a different perspective on navigating careers, research challenges, and leadership in a rapidly evolving field.
