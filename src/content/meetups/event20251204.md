​Ever walked into an interview or networking event and struggled to clearly articulate what makes you exceptional? You're not alone. In today's competitive job market, it's not always the most skilled who rise—it's the ones who can communicate their value with clarity and confidence.

​Join us for this interactive masterclass with career coach Joanna Ghayad, where you'll learn to transform your technical achievements into a compelling value statement that gets you noticed, respected, and sought after.

**​In this one-hour session, you'll**:

- ​Uncover your hidden strengths through guided self-assessment exercises

- ​Learn from real-world case studies of successful career positioning

- ​Create your personal unique value statement

- Gain frameworks for confidently presenting yourself in interviews and networking settings

- ​Receive tools and resources to continue refining your professional narrative

**​Who should attend**: Scientists and bioinformaticians who are navigating the job market, seeking career advancement, or want to position themselves more effectively in their field.

​Walk away with a clear, memorable statement of your unique value and the confidence to share it in any professional setting. No more rambling about achievements or underselling yourself—just authentic, compelling communication that opens doors.

**​About the speaker**: Joanna Ghayad is a certified career coach with 15+ years of experience in pharma, academia, and biotech. She specializes in helping scientists build portfolio careers and communicate their value with confidence. Joanna created a leadership development program at BMS that's now in its 5th year and has completed over 500 hours of coaching.
