## About

Bioinformatics Sisters is a networking and mentoring platform that connects women in bioinformatics. Our committee facilitates meaningful connections by matching participants based on their shared goals, skills, and preferences.

## What We Offer

- Personalized mentor-mentee matching
- Peer networking opportunities
- Professional development resources
- Community support and guidance

## Join the Network

Whether you're looking for mentorship or want to mentor others, Bioinformatics Sisters provides a supportive space to grow your career and build lasting professional relationships.
