## About

The Podcast committee produces **A Coffee with CompBio**, a bioinformatics podcast where members discuss all things computational biology. Now in its second season, the podcast features conversations about career paths, what drew guests to bioinformatics, and the challenges and surprises they've encountered along the way.

## What We Cover

- **Career Journeys**: How professionals found their way into bioinformatics
- **Field Insights**: Current trends and developments in computational biology
- **Challenges & Surprises**: Real talk about the ups and downs of working in the field
- **Practical Tips**: Advice for computational biologists at all career stages
