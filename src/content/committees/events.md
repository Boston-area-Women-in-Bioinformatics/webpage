## About

The Events committee brings our community together through engaging professional and social gatherings. We organize a diverse range of events that foster learning, networking, and community building.

## Types of Events

- **Technical Workshops**: Hands-on learning sessions on bioinformatics tools and techniques
- **Networking Events**: Casual gatherings to connect with peers and colleagues
- **Speaker Series**: Talks from leaders in the field
- **Career Development**: Panels and workshops focused on professional growth
- **Social Events**: Community-building activities and celebrations
