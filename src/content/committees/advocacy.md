## About

The Advocacy committee is dedicated to advancing equity and inclusion within the field of bioinformatics. We work to identify systemic challenges and promote data-driven strategies that create positive change in our community.

## Our Mission

Our committee focuses on:

- Identifying barriers to entry and advancement in bioinformatics
- Developing advocacy resources and toolkits
- Promoting inclusive practices in research and industry
- Supporting underrepresented groups in the field

## Get Involved

If you're passionate about creating a more equitable bioinformatics community, we'd love to have you join our efforts!
