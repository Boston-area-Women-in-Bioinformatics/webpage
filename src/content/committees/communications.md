## About

The Communications committee ensures effective information flow within our organization and to the broader community. We manage multiple communication channels and coordinate messaging across committees.

## Our Responsibilities

- Managing social media presence
- Creating newsletters and announcements
- Coordinating cross-committee communications
- Maintaining public relations
- Developing communication policies and guidelines

## Communication Channels

We maintain active presence on:

- Social media platforms
- Email newsletters
- Website updates
- Community forums

Our goal is to keep our community informed, engaged, and connected.
