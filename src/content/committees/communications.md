## About

The Communications committee ensures effective information flow within our organization and to the broader community. We manage multiple communication channels and maintain the organization's website.

## Our Responsibilities

### Communications

- Managing social media presence, including weekly LinkedIn content
- Creating and distributing quarterly newsletters (every 3 months at minimum)
- Maintaining our member mailing list across platforms
- Maintaining public relations
- Developing communication policies and guidelines

### Website & Blog

- **Website Maintenance**: Keep the organization's website updated with current events, blogs, newsletters, resources, and member highlights.
- **Blog Organization**: Curate and coordinate blog content from members and guest contributors, including managing submission deadlines and editorial scheduling.
- **Site Innovation**: Identify opportunities for new tools, layouts, and interactive elements that improve functionality and reflect the evolving needs of the community.
- **Amplifying Committee Voices**: Partner with other committees to support and showcase their initiatives online while improving user experience.
- **User Experience**: Lead new website features that enhance transparency, accessibility, and inclusivity in both design and content.
- **Brand Consistency and Visual Design**: Ensure consistent brand identity across digital platforms and create graphics for social media and promotional materials.

## Communication Channels

We maintain an active presence on:

- LinkedIn
- Email newsletters
- Our website
- Slack

## 2026 Goals

- Publish a quarterly newsletter (every 6 months at minimum)
- Maintain and grow our member mailing list from event platforms
- Keep the website current and up to date
- Hold monthly committee meetings
- Post weekly content on LinkedIn, including:
  - Reposts and community highlights
  - New blog announcements
  - Event advertisements and recaps
  - Organization announcements
  - Member spotlights
- Publish regular blog posts with scheduled submission deadlines
