About Us

Support. Learn. Share. Network.

We are women++ in the fields of bioinformatics, computational biology, biostatistics, data science and informatics. As a community, we aim to empower and encourage all women++ bioinformaticians through regular meetings, networking events and workshops. We seek to give a platform to women++ professionals, including those members of other underrepresented groups in science, such as BIPOC, the LGBTQIA+ community, and people with disabilities.

Mission

The Women in Bioinformatics Group’s mission is to advance and support women++ in the fields of bioinformatics, computational biology, biostatistics, data science and informatics.

Our vision

In order to accomplish our mission, we are focusing on three pillars of support: professional development, scientific communication, and community.

  • 1

    Professional Development

    To provide opportunities and an environment conducive to the development of professional skills, empowerment, and networking within and outside of our community.

  • 2

    Scientific Communication

    To exchange ideas and knowledge with others and provide a forum for speakers of all experience levels.

  • 3

    Community

    To offer a supportive community that promotes equality, works towards reducing discrimination, and welcomes all.

Code of Conduct

This meetup group is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or technology choices. We do not tolerate harassment in any form. Anyone violating these rules may be sanctioned or banned from attending at the discretion of the conference organizers.
