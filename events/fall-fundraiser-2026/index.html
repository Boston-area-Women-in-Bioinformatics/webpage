Boston Women in Bioinformatics

2026 Fall Fundraiser

Join Boston Women in Bioinformatics for an evening of honest, inspiring conversation with speakers who took their bioinformatics training somewhere unexpected. Through personal stories and candid reflection, they'll explore the breadth of careers this field can lead to, what it takes to recognize and seize an opportunity, and what they wish they'd known earlier in their journey. Stay tuned for details on the date, location, and speakers.

This is one of our rare ticketed events — your support goes directly toward sustaining the BWiB community and the programming, networking, and resources we provide year-round. Thank you for investing in this community.
